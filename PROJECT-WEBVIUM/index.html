PROJECT-WEBVIUM

Webvium is a weightless android app that has a size of 600KB. It's Fast, Material Design and Full-Featured Web Browser for Android. And its absolutely free to download and without any ads.


CUSTOMIZATION

Use the Interface settings, to change the app theme to dark or light. Add a little curved on edge of screen or make your favorite photo or wallpaper as app main background. Redefined your browsing experience.

LIGHTWEIGHT & FASTER

Webvium is programmed and developed to be the most lightweight app possible. Lightweight means faster installation time. Faster download time. And better browsing experience. Share this app to your friends within fraction of a seconds.

MATERIAL DESIGN

Immerse yourself in one of the most awesome browser fully material based designed. The colors and icons are carefully picked. And it uses one of the coolest fonts ever The Maven Pro.

TOTAL BROWSING CONTROL

Here you can customize your all of web settings preferences if you wanted anytime. Webvium guaranteed that your the admin(super-user) of the app.

INCOMPARIBLE PRIVACY

Protect your privacy, Webvium does not collect any kind of information/data Your browsing activity is at highest security and anonymous.

YOU IN CONTROL

Yeah, the only browser that allows you to backup all of your data settings preferences, history, searches, bookmarks and download list. And export it when you needed.

MANAGABLE SPACE

It does not mean Webvium is lightweight we wont care about your storage free space. Here we revolutionize how browser should look like. In Webvium Manage Space you can see what part of webvium tool so much space and delete them whenever you want.
